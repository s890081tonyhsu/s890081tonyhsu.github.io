Wants to do something but always do nothing.

On high school, I join the team to compete with every high students, this competition is to do a research on a person we meet. I was inspired when making a website to show our research. After that time, I know about jQuery, Single-Page and some other names.

After the graduation from high school, I'm trying to learn something to follow the steps of that classmate which creates all the website for our team. But after I collected the data, I still knows nothing to do next.

i++'s story

When the day comes to my freshman in university, I make my determination to join Student Government in NCHU (NCHU-SG). I always remember the time which I tells the header of secretary department that I want to join imformation department. But then, I can't follow the activity that SG created. So I'm just only writing C language myself.

One day, someone's post attracted me, the person who is front of me reply '++', I reply 'i++', then the person who reply 'echo i' start focus on my appearence. At the second semester, I was invited by him to join imformation team (At that time, this team is not upgrated to department).

Ability enhancement

At the half of my freshman, Our class was receive by a assignment. The teacher of Computer Science tells us to make a program for letter appearence counting. She only use six weeks to lecture us C language. I even knows nothing about the pointer, linklist and data entropy. After I go to get some extension from classmates, I know how to do and making a menu for that program although I think it's useless after that.

At the second semester starts, Our team was request to make a website for the activity. It was after the time that TPA won the champion on League of Legends. So all we had to do was making BESG website to handle the activity's registration and post the activity's imformation.

I really thought that I can get more experience from this activity, but the truth is that I only learned how to generate a website screenshot for other teammates. The really learning and enhancement is at the summer vacation after my freshman.

So...what's now

Now I am the student at the department of Electrical Engineering in NCHU. But sometimes I participate in front end works of some projects.
